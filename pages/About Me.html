About Me

My name is Andrew. I love computers. Ever since 7th grade, I’ve always orientated myself around computers and how they function. In 8th grade, I completed my first build of a computer and developed knowledge of the nuances of each component and their functions. Later into my high school career, I find myself interested in the fabrication process and the architectural design of microprocessors. I have taken many classes such as AP computer science and multiple Udemy courses on C++. So far, I have learned to program in three different languages including Java, C++, and html. At home, I love to indulge in projects that include computers and being able to see firsthand how they can be utilized for a multitude of purposes.
